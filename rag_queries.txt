Provias Nacional is a Special Project for National Transport Infrastructure, created as a temporary Executing Unit under the Ministry of Transport, Communications, Housing, and Construction. It operates with technical, administrative, and financial autonomy, responsible for the preparation, management, administration, and execution of transport infrastructure projects related to the non-concessioned National Road Network. It also handles the planning, management, and control of activities and economic resources used for the maintenance and safety of roads and bridges in the non-concessioned National Road Network. Additionally, Provias Nacional issues authorizations for the use of rights of way and special vehicles throughout the National Road Network. It is considered an Entity within the scope of the State's contracting regulations, with its Executive Director acting as the head of the Entity, competent to approve, authorize, and supervise procurement processes for goods, services, and works.
